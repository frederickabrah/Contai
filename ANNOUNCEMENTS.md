```

✨ Get: Thread + 5 Tweets + Case Study + LinkedIn Post + 
       Instagram Caption + 5 Questions + 3 Visual Prompts

WHY IT'S FIRE: Turn ONE idea into 2 weeks of content in 30 seconds.
```

### **2. 🎭 Vibe Control (6 MOOD MODES)**
```
Before v2.0: Generic AI voice
After v2.0:  YOUR voice, YOUR style

node contai.js tweets "topic" linkedin --vibe=sarcastic

✨ 6 Vibes: Aggressive, Sarcastic, Empathetic, Scientific, 
           Minimalist, Helpful

WHY IT'S FIRE: Sound like YOU, not a robot.
```

```
Before v2.0: Generate from static topics

✨ Fetches from: Hacker News, Reddit, Google Trends, RSS feeds

WHY IT'S FIRE: Your content is always timely, always relevant.
```
